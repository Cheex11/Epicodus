about us:

Welcome to fit to print, your graphic designers who dress you up and take you to print, but without the expensive price tag dangling from your sleeves!

Fit to print started in 1986 with a mission to not only meet clients' needs, but to exceed them, both in quality and price! You can try on a unique look, play with several styles - even take part in your makeover!

Our printing reach extends nationwide, securing for you the best price and turnaround times.

From logo design to newsletters, to pocket folders and even book publishing, we can take your project from inception to completion, every step of the way.

about laura:

Laura Wilson is the owner of fit to print, a graphic design, print and promotions company that has been in business in Dallas since 1986. Laura attended Ursuline Academy and graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a degree in journalism with honors in 1983.

Laura worked for The Dallas Morning News in the paste-up department and later as a reporter for The Duncanville Suburban where she won a Texas Press Association award for best feature story. It was at this family owned newspaper where Laura began enjoying design.

When Apple launched its MacPlus, Laura’s husband, Tracy, encouraged her to pursue her vision, combining print and art via computer. His support, coupled with her enthusiasm for creativity, hard work and commitment to deliver what she promises, grew fit to print exponentially!

For the Council, fit to print has designed and printed the Parade of Stars Awards Gala, businessWORKS!, Harvesting Partnerships and Power to Potential for the last 8 years. While setting up her first booth at businessWORKS! Laura met the Council’s Emilia Menthe, who referred fit to print to its currently largest account.

Laura delights in family gatherings, entertaining and especially traveling – since her youngest son, Cody, lives in Portland, Oregon!
